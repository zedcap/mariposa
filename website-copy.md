### 6.1 Origins

ADAPTED — verbatim from the brand pager.

> Mariposa is hand-rolled in Jember, East Java. A region whose rich cigar-making heritage dates back to 1859. It is also home to Pak Abdul Kahar Muzakir, a cigar aficionado and industry veteran who has dedicated his life to a singular pursuit: capturing the essence of Cuban tradition by cultivating Havana seeds in Indonesian soil.
>
> The wrapper, binder, and filler are all born from Havana-seed tobacco that has spent nearly two decades acclimatising to Jember's volcanic earth. The result is a perfect marriage of flavour profiles, blending the signature spice and oiliness of Cuban genetics with Java's signature earthy sweetness.
>
> Patience is central to the craft. Honouring the tradition of Cuba's *escogida*, each leaf type is sorted and fermented separately for 24 months, then rested for another twelve. That is close to three years from leaf to finished cigar, a slow maturation that yields rich flavours and an undeniably smooth smoke.
>
> Mariposa cigars are entirely hand-rolled by a dedicated team of female rollers, mirroring Cuba's revered *torcedoras*, many of whom come from families that have worked tobacco for generations. Using long-filler, three-part construction, and *entubado* bunching for a flawlessly even draw, each cigar is finished with a classic Cuban triple cap. Every single stick is draw-tested and inspected by hand before it leaves the workshop, guaranteeing a flawless smoking experience.

### 6.2 The founder's story

ADAPTED — verbatim from the brand pager.

> Mariposa's story begins with a journey. Driven by a lifelong taste for cigars, founder Stephane Zermatten followed his curiosity to the tobacco fields of East Java.
>
> What he discovered inspired him to craft a blend for his own humidor and closest friends. Yet, as word spread and a wider circle grew curious, he knew it couldn't remain a private reserve. The brand was born to bring this unique cigar to a growing community in Bali and beyond.
>
> Today, Mariposa captures the rich, multi-cultural soul of cigars, giving friends around the world the perfect excuse to light up, slow down, and share a good conversation. Whatever the hour.

**Closing line** (ADAPTED — verbatim kicker from the brand pager, set in italic serif)
